Jozi Chamber Of Commerce Site-Plan

Jozi Chamber Of Commerce

Jozi is a nickname for a South Africa province named Johannesburg, the name was given during apartheid, it was given as a sign of unity during ahard time in our country, so the name is apropriate in uniting businesses and giving them an opportunity to assist each other to grow, It facilitates networking opportunities among members through directories and event listings, encouraging collaboration and business partnerships. It serves as a valuable resource and communication platform that supports the growth and success of local businesses while strengthening the chamber's role as an advocate, connector, and facilitator within the community.

Site Purpose

The Jozi Chamber Of Commerce offers a wealth of resources, including business guides, articles, templates, and links to government resources, to support local businesses in their growth and development. It facilitates networking opportunities among members through directories and event listings, encouraging collaboration and business partnerships. It serves as a valuable resource and communication platform that supports the growth and success of local businesses while strengthening the chamber's role as an advocate, connector, and facilitator within the community.

Color Schema

light Blue (#A8D1DF)

Heading Text: Use light blue for the headings and subheadings throughout the website. This color choice provides a professional and authoritative appearance it is also assosiated with trustworthiness and reliability to important content.

Navigation Menu: Apply light blue to the text of menu items in the website's navigation bar. This helps users easily identify and access different sections of the website.

Call-to-Action Buttons: Apply light blue to the background or border of call-to-action buttons to make them stand out and encourage user interaction.

purple (#DB98F7)

Paragraph Text: purple is an excellent choice for the main body text and paragraphs. It provides good readability and contrast against a white background.

Footer Background: Apply purple as the background color for the website's footer section. This can create a visual separation between the main content and footer.

Borders and Dividers: Employ purple for borders and dividers between different sections or content blocks to provide structure and organization.
